# Delete Your Pailin Abroad Account

Last Updated: July 7, 2026

If you would like to delete your Pailin Abroad account and associated personal data, you can request deletion using either of the methods below.

## How to request deletion

### Option 1: Email us

Send an email to <a href="mailto:contact@pailinabroad.com?subject=Pailin%20Abroad%20Account%20Deletion%20Request">contact@pailinabroad.com</a> with the subject line:

`Pailin Abroad Account Deletion Request`

Please include the email address used for your Pailin Abroad account so we can locate it.

### Option 2: Use our contact form

Submit your request here:

<a href="https://www.pailinabroad.com/contact">https://www.pailinabroad.com/contact</a>

Please write `Account Deletion Request` in your message and include the email address used for your account.

## What happens after you request deletion

Once we verify your request, we will delete your Pailin Abroad account and the personal data associated with that account, subject to any data we must retain for legal, security, fraud-prevention, accounting, or compliance reasons.

We aim to complete verified deletion requests within 30 days.

## Data we generally delete

- Your account profile information
- Your login and authentication records connected to the account
- Your lesson progress and learning history
- Comments, submissions, or other content associated with your account, unless retention is required for safety, abuse prevention, or legal reasons
- Other personal data linked to your use of Pailin Abroad

## Data we may retain for a limited period

- Payment, transaction, or tax records where retention is required by law or accounting rules
- Security logs and fraud-prevention records
- Records needed to enforce our legal rights, resolve disputes, or comply with legal obligations

If we retain any data for these reasons, we will keep it only for as long as necessary for that purpose.

## Important note about subscriptions

Deleting your Pailin Abroad account does not automatically cancel billing handled by a third-party payment provider or app marketplace. If you have an active subscription, please cancel it through the same platform you used to purchase it.

## Need help?

If you cannot access your account or need help with the deletion request process, contact us at <a href="mailto:contact@pailinabroad.com">contact@pailinabroad.com</a>.
